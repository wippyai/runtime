About This Demo

This dashboard demonstrates process lifecycle management with Pony Runtime's actor model:

  • Start Process: Creates a parent process that spawns a linked child process
  • Cancel: Sends a cancellation signal allowing processes to clean up before exiting
  • Terminate: Immediately kills processes without cleanup
  • Process Relationships: Parent-child links demonstrate error propagation and supervision
